About
Uwikunda Joel is a Rwandan skilled bassist, Music Producer and dedicated music educator with a passion for nurturing talent. A graduate of the Rwanda School of Creative Arts and Music, he has performed at various local and international events, showcasing his deep musicality and stage experience. Since 2019, Joel has committed himself to teaching, first as a volunteer and now as a Senior Guitar Instructor. He continues to inspire and guide the next generation of musicians with excellence, creativity, and heart.
